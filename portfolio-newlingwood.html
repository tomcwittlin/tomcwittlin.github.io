New & Lingwood

ProjectBrand Film

AgencyFolk

Role/sCreative Direction

Year2016

Arguably the most radical and original tailors on Jermyn St. New and Lingwood have remained at the forefront of traditional menswear 'with a twist' since their beginning 150 years ago.

After working on the brand strategy we wanted to find a way to express the combined sense of humour and sartorial elegance at the root of New and Lingwood for years, it was agreed a well shot film would be the most effective immediate approach.

We wanted to tell 2 parts to the story; the first being the more 'serious and sensible' nature of the brand, (displaying images of craftmanship and production), and the second being the wry nod to Great British sarcasm and wit (through bold prints, colours and movement).
